The birth of Aura

Aproveitando seu tempo na Europa, também participou de cursos em Londres e Viena ministrados por professores da Academia Make Up For Ever, e em Copenhagen com Byorne Sorressen, maquiador oficial da MAC Cosmetics.

I always wanted to have different look, wear a makeup, look good in my own way. Makeup was one thing i tried myself or got some information from parlor. But ended up in a disaster look.Then i asked myself, is it something so difficult. Cant i also look like "HER"in this outfit today. the answer was "NO- not possible".

Then, after talking to few people I realised that we all want to look good in some or the other way and always look upto celebrities for thier style, clothes, makeup , accessories etc.

And for that, makeup plays a very important role.For most of us It is always something to relate with parlours. But there is not much of a ratio of people who are satisfied with parlor when it comes to makeup.
